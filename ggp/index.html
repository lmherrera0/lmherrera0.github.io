Verification Markers

Every claim in your content gets one of five markers

CONFIRMED

Fact verified with a cited source. Tier 1–3, full citation, no red flags.

GAP

Missing critical information requiring completion before publishing.

INFERENCE

Assumption declared — reasoned from confirmed facts but not directly sourced.

UNVERIFIED

Low-quality or unverifiable source requiring corroboration.

RISK

Reputational, legal, or credibility concern flagged for review.
