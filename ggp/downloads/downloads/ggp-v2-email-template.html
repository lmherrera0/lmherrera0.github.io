What Is GGP?

GroundedGate Protocol is a discipline for verifying every claim in professional communications. It addresses a critical risk: Large Language Models (LLMs) can produce confident-sounding content that is completely fabricated.

GGP helps by:
  • [CONFIRMED] Marking verified facts with sources and tiers
  • [INFERENCE] Labeling assumptions explicitly
  • [GAP] Declaring missing information
  • [RISK] Flagging reputation or legal concerns
  • Running Devil's Advocate analysis before delivery

Why Now?

In professional contexts—client reports, executive communications, regulatory submissions—unverified content creates significant risk. GGP makes verification visible and mandatory.

Result: You get control. You know what's confirmed, what's inferred, and what gaps exist—before you hit send.

Core Principle

"Nothing passes unverified."
